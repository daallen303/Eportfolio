Infotunes

I wanted to try my hands at Python and I wanted to pull information from some database and do something cool with that. Since I love music I decided to make it an application that pulls information from Spotify. I found a cool module called Spotipy and used it to collect information on users, artists, albums, playlists, songs etc. Here is a couple examples, one graphing the Beatles albums over time with plotly, and the other an example of graphing the key of songs in the album "Cleopatra" by The Lumineers.
