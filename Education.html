Studies


In May of 2018 I graduated from Colorado Mesa University with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and a minor in Mathematics. During my years at Colorado Mesa I prided myself on being a good student, making my way onto the Dean's list and becoming a member of Upsilon Pi Epsilon honor society and the Nationional Society of Leadership and Success. I also wrote four articles for Horizon magazine (the schools semi annual magazine) in the 2015 winter and 2016 spring editions on various topics. I have a strong background in Object Oriented Programming with experience in PHP, Javascript, Meteor, Laravel, Bootstrap, Mongo, SQL, Java, C++, Visual Basic. My favorite projects are ones that challenge me and require me to learn something new and useful.

My favorite classes while attending Mesa were Calculus 2, Web Design 3, Java, Software Engineering, Mythology, Art History, and robotics.
