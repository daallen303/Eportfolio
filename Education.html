Studies


I graduated from Wheat Ridge high school in 2013 and went on to Colorado Mesa University where I graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and a minor in Mathematics. I was undecided going into my freshman year and after an intro to web developement course I got hooked on coding and have been studying various languages ever since. I have always enjoyed finding solutions to math problems and I think the problem soving involved in coding has been what keeps my interest. I also love the ability to be creative and artistic in a way that uses skills typically seen as more analog.

My favorite classes while attending Mesa were Calculus 2, Web Design 3, Java, Software Engineering, Mythology, Art History, and robotics.

Awards and extra curriculars

During my years at Colorado Mesa I prided myself on being a good student, making my way onto the Dean's list and becoming a member of the Nationional Society of Leadership and Success, and Upsilon Pi Epsilon honor society. I also wrote four articles for Horizon magazine (the schools semi annual magazine) in the 2015 winter and 2016 spring editions on various topics. I enjoyed participating in intramnural athletics including ultimate frisbee, basketball, soccer, and flag football and tried to make it to as many campus events as possible, especially when free food or music was invlolved.
